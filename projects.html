Autodesk Inventor Ansys DFMEA

Structural and Thermal Evaluation of Electronic Power Conditioner (EPC) Unit for Space Payload

Designed three iterations of an Electronic Power Conditioner (EPC) unit for microwave space payloads using Autodesk Inventor. Carried out comprehensive structural and thermal simulations, including modal, quasi-static, random response, steady-state, and transient thermal analyses using Ansys Workbench. Performed DFMEA to identify and mitigate structural and thermal risks. Topology optimization reduced weight by 47% while maintaining structural integrity. Analyzed using a combination of 1-D, 2-D, and 3-D elements for different parts.
