Structural and Thermal Evaluation of Electronic Power Conditioner (EPC) Unit for Space Payload

Autodesk Inventor Ansys DFMEA
  • Designed three EPC unit iterations for microwave space payloads using Autodesk Inventor
  • Comprehensive structural and thermal simulations (modal, quasi-static, random response, steady-state, transient thermal) in Ansys Workbench
  • DFMEA performed to identify and mitigate structural and thermal risks

  • Analysis using a combination of 1-D, 2-D, and 3-D elements for different parts
